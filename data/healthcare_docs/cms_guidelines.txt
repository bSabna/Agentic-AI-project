HEALTHCARE PAYMENT GUIDELINES - CMS

1. CLAIM COST THRESHOLDS
- Average outpatient visit cost: $150 - $500
- Average inpatient stay per day: $2,000 - $5,000
- Emergency room visit: $500 - $3,000
- Surgical procedure (minor): $3,000 - $10,000
- Surgical procedure (major): $10,000 - $50,000
- Prescription drugs (monthly): $50 - $500

2. FRAUD INDICATORS
- Billing for services not rendered
- Upcoding: billing higher cost service than provided
- Duplicate claims for same service
- Unbundling: billing separately for bundled services
- Claims with no matching diagnosis code
- Provider billing more than 24 hours of services per day
- Identical claims submitted within 30 days

3. ICD-10 COMMON CODES
- Z00.00: General adult medical examination
- J06.9: Acute upper respiratory infection
- I10: Essential hypertension
- E11.9: Type 2 diabetes without complications
- M54.5: Low back pain
- J18.9: Pneumonia unspecified
- I21.9: Acute myocardial infarction
- K21.0: GERD with esophagitis
- F32.9: Major depressive disorder
- G43.909: Migraine unspecified

4. RISK ADJUSTMENT RULES
- HCC codes must be supported by clinical documentation
- Diagnoses must be confirmed by licensed provider
- Chronic conditions must be documented every year
- Risk scores above 3.0 require additional review
- Medicare Advantage plans use CMS-HCC model

5. PAYMENT ACCURACY RULES
- All claims must have valid NPI number
- Procedure codes must match diagnosis codes
- Date of service cannot be in the future
- Claims must be submitted within 365 days of service
- Coordination of benefits required for dual coverage
